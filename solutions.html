PLUM iTV
SOLUTIONS OVERVIEW

Plum iTV offers diverse and complete end to end software solution providing operators to become interactive TV provider.

PRODUCT INFO

Plum solution covers complete end-to-end domains from provider's headend to subscriber's homes.

With its modular structure, scalable design, simple architecture and integration capability, it perfectly balances modern functional requirements with reasonable investments ensuring operator high level of performance and security.

Plum is great solution for all interactive TV forms you want to offer to end users. Starting from basic functions and features (Live TV, EPG, VOD), providing operators with a great potential for expanding service range and delivering high quality TV service.

Plum solutions is able to work on wide range of devices (STB, PC, tablet, smart phones, Smart TV)

PLUM iTV FOR PROVIDERS

Plum is meant for ISP, Cable and other operators who want to provide interactive TV to their users. It is a cloud based streaming service built in HTML5 standard. Provide your users an easy to use app which enables all of the functions you wish to offer on their TV screen.
